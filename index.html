Welcome to Space!

Journey with us across the planets of our solar system — from the scorching surface of Mercury to the icy winds of Neptune.

🌞 Sun

The star at the center of our solar system. It gives light and life to all planets.

🪐 Saturn

Famous for its stunning rings, Saturn is a gas giant with dozens of moons.

🌍 Earth

Our home! The only known planet to support life, with vast oceans and diverse life forms.

🔴 Mars

The red planet. Scientists believe Mars may have once had water — and maybe life!

🧊 Neptune

The farthest known planet, with powerful winds and a beautiful blue hue.

🟡 Venus

Earth’s sister planet — hot, toxic, and bright in the sky.

☿️ Mercury

The smallest and fastest planet, closest to the Sun.

🟠 Jupiter

The largest planet, known for its giant red storm and many moons.
